Alarmio is a simple open source alarm clock which attempts to implement many useful features while following regular design standards to ensure that it is quick and intuitive to use.

<b>Functionality:</b>

<ul>
<li>set alarms, timers, or use the built-in stopwatch</li>
<li>repeat alarms on a weekly basis</li>
<li>specify a ringtone from either installed sounds or an internet stream</li>
<li>notify yourself to go to sleep a set time before your first alarm</li>
<li>gradually increase brightness / volume to wake you up</li>
<li>choose a custom header / background image to display</li>
<li>light/dark themes, with a sunrise-sunset scheduling option</li>
</ul>
